About Me


Hi.

I'm Tom McDonald, I'm from Leeds, UK and I currently work as an Analyst at ENGIE,
a multinational energy firm. The focus of my current role is forecasting, and I implement
a variety of different time-series analysis methods in order to model the future levels
of a number of renewable energy tariffs which UK suppliers are liable for, such as the RO,
FiT and CfD FiT, as well as BSUoS, the costs associated with balancing the National Grid.

Whilst my job involves a good deal of Python, I enjoy coding in my spare time and tend to keep
everything I'm up to on my GitHub. I'm also a massive sports fan, so much of my spare time is spent
commiserating over how badly the Raptors & Blue Jays are playing (maybe not so much the Raps this year...).
